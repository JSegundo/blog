## Hey, I'm Segundo! 👋

⚙️ Software developer \
Currently based in LATAM

I'm a fullstack engineer who likes to work close to the product.

I've helped build startup products from scratch and worked on improving complex systems already in production.

I mostly work with Node.js, React, TypeScript and AWS. I enjoy turning ambiguous ideas into working features, simplifying messy parts of a system, and improving things incrementally instead of overengineering.

Recently, I've been exploring LLMs and modern AI systems, focusing on how they can be integrated into real workflows and useful tools.
